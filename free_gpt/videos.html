How scanning works

Scanning opens an active YouTube tab for better results.

A completion banner will appear when done — click “Close Tab” to dismiss.

Safe to close manually after 5–10 seconds if needed.

Our testing shows that active tabs load more content and provide significantly better scan results compared to background processing. The tab will show a completion banner when scanning finishes, and you can close it using the “Close Tab” button or manually after a few seconds.

For Channels (RSS): feeds are downloaded and scanned periodically. First configure your YouTube settings on the Options page so we can import your channel list.
